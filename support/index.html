FAQ

Q: How do I reset my game data?
A: Go to Settings → Reset All Data within the app.

Q: Does the game require internet?
A: No. Snake Legend is fully offline. Game Center features require internet for syncing leaderboards and achievements.

Q: How do I restore Premium Unlock?
A: Open the in-app upgrade sheet and tap Restore Purchases.

Q: What devices are supported?
A: iPhone running iOS 16.0 or later.
