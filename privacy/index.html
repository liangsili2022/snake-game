Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026 · Effective: March 19, 2026

Solo Melody ("we", "us") develops and operates Snake Legend. We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

This App is a purely offline casual game:

  • We do not collect any personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.).
  • We do not require login, registration, or account creation.
  • We do not collect device information, location data, contacts, or any other sensitive information.
  • Game scores, achievements, settings, and other data are stored only on your device locally and are never uploaded to any server.

2. Game Center & Purchases

This App integrates Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center is provided and managed by Apple. Related data processing is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not collect any additional personal information through Game Center.

This App also uses Apple's In-App Purchase system for a one-time Premium Unlock. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment details.

3. Third-Party SDKs

This App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, push notification, social, or tracking SDKs. We do not share any user data with third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for all ages (4+) and contains no violence, adult content, or inappropriate material. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Storage & Security

All game data is stored locally on the user's device and is never transmitted over the network. Deleting the App will remove all local data.

6. Your Rights

You can clear all local game data at any time via Settings → Reset All Data within the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted on this page. Significant changes will be notified within the App.
